

# Code of Conduct

Strength for Mentorship & Education is committed to creating a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all contributors.

## Our Values
- Respect for youth, families, and community members.
- Collaboration rooted in kindness and patience.
- Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or harmful behavior.
- Commitment to accessibility and clarity in all contributions.

## Expected Behavior
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the mission.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation.
- Discriminatory language or actions.
- Sharing private or sensitive information.
- Disruptive or intentionally harmful contributions.

## Reporting Concerns
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the project maintainer at the email listed in the repository profile.
